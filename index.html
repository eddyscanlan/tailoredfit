Frequently Asked Questions

What is ATS?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software companies use to scan and filter resumes before a human reviews them. It checks for keyword matches, formatting, and relevance to the job description.

How does the free check work?

Paste your resume and the job description. TailoredFit extracts keywords from both, compares them, and shows your match percentage plus which keywords are missing. This is completely free.

Your resume rewritten by AI to naturally include all the missing keywords the ATS is looking for. You get the optimized text, a PDF download, and copy-to-clipboard. One-time payment, no subscription.

Is my data safe?

Your resume text is processed in your browser and saved to your browser's local storage. It is never uploaded to a server or stored in a database. Payment is processed securely by Stripe.
